Corrate - Heart Rate Training

You can discuss Heart Rate Training with fellow people at facebook.com/HeartRateTraining.

To use the Corrate Heart Rate Training app, abbreviated HRT, you need a heart rate device. The app has been tested with the Polar H7 and the Wahoo BLUE HR, but should normally work with any bluetooth smart heart rate device.

The HRT app communicates with your heart rate device through the bluetooth smart protocol. You should therefore make sure bluetooth is enabled on your iphone, ipod or ipad. To enable or disable bluetooth, Open the Control Center, by swiping your finger up from the bottom of the screen. If the blutooth button is coloured gray, push it so the colour changes to white - this means bluetooth is enabled. To close the Control Center, swipe down.

First row

Displays your heart rate in beats per minute (bpm). The heart icon beats in the same rate as your heart. If no heart rate has been received from the heart rate device, the heart icon stops beating and the heart rate is displayed as "...". You can click on the bpm button to switch to % view. Your heart rate will then be displayed as a percent of your maximum heart rate (see next row).

Second row

Displays your resting heart rate (HRrest), your heart rate reserve (HRreserve) and your maximum heart rate (HRmax). You can enter the HRrest and HRmax manually. If the app registers a lower/larger value than you have entered, your values will be overwritten unless you have locked the fields with the padlocks. To do a fresh measurement of your HRrest, manually specify a high HRrest To do a fresh measurement of your HRmax, manually specify a low HRmax. Then let the app do the rest while you relax or race at maximum effort! HRreserve is the difference between HRmax and HRrest. An increasing HRreserve is normally a sign that your physical condition is improving.

Third row

Measures your recovery heart rate (HRrecovery). Press the "Start recovery" button to start measuring how many beats your heart rate drops in the specified number of seconds. An increasing HRrecovery is normally a sign that your physical condition is improving.

Fourth row

Controls the audio. Decrease or increase the interval between each audio cue in seconds by touching the + and - signs. You can also enter a value manually. Press the speaker icon to turn the sound on or off. Use the speed indicator slider to specify how fast the voice should talk.

Note that the audio cue interval will be limited by how often a heart rate is received from the heart rate device. For example, specifying an audio cue interval of 0 seconds will give you any new heart rate measurement from the device as soon as it is available.

Fifth row

Displays information about the heart rate device connection. If the app loses the connection to your heart rate device, the heart rate information will stop while the app tries to reconnect.

Initial connection with device

The app will automatically search and connect to the heart rate device the first time. The next time you start the app, it will try to reconnect to the same device. This is to make sure you are not connecting to another persons heart rate device when exercising together with other people. The id of the connected heart rate device is displayed when connected.

Connection with a different device

To connect to another device, make sure the current one is not available by putting it at a large physical distance. Also wait for a while to make your iphone/ipad/ipod give up on the connection. Make sure that other apps are not using it for example. Then press the delete icon. The app will then automatically try to connect, first to any other heart rate devices already connected, then it will search for a new heart rate device.

Notes on heart rate devices

Note that heart rate devices can continue to send heart rate information for a substantial amount of time, even when it is removed from your body or is disconnected from its strap.

Also note that to avoid battery drainage, it is best to stop the app completely when you are not using it. Do this by double tapping the home button to bring up the multi-tasking view, then swipe up on the screenshot of the app you want to exit.

What does Corrate mean?

Finding a good name for anything is always difficult! I chose the name Corrate because Cor is latin for heart.
