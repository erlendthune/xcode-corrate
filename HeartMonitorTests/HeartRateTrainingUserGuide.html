Setup and Requirements

The Corrate Heart Rate Training app (HRT) requires a Bluetooth heart rate device. It has been tested with the Polar H7, H9, Wahoo BLUE HR, and Garmin Forerunner 255 but should work with most Bluetooth smart devices.

Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your iPhone, iPod, or iPad via the Control Center.

Different Views

Swipe left and right to switch between different views:

First View: Heart Rate Readout Configuration

In this view, you can configure how frequently the app reads your heart rate and select between beats per minute (BPM) or a percentage of your maximum heart rate.

First Row

Displays heart rate (bpm or %) and a beating heart icon. A button lets you switch between displaying the heart rate in beats per minute and a percentage of your maximum heart rate.

Second Row

Shows resting heart rate (HRrest), heart rate reserve (HRreserve), and maximum heart rate (HRmax). Values can be locked.

Third Row

Measures recovery heart rate (HRrecovery) with the "Start recovery" button.

Fourth Row

Controls audio readouts and interval.

Fifth Row

Controls voice speed.

Sixth Row

Displays heart rate device connection status.

Second View: Fartlek Training

Fartlek training lets you alternate between high and low heart rate intensities:

  • Specify a warmup time.
  • Set the number of repetitions for speed intervals.
  • Define an upper heart rate to reach during high-intensity phases.
  • Set a lower heart rate for recovery phases.

Once you start, the app guides you:

  1. Complete the warmup phase.
  2. Increase speed until you reach the upper heart rate.
  3. Slow down until you reach the lower heart rate.
  4. Repeat for the specified number of repetitions.

Third View: Heart Rate Zones

This view displays different heart rate zones. Clicking on a zone provides a detailed explanation of its benefits and training effects.

Device Connection

Initial Connection

The app automatically searches for your heart rate device. If your device does not appear in the list and it is a chest strap, please check that the battery is working. If you want to use your watch to broadcast its heart rate, ensure that broadcasting is enabled. Refer to the user manual for instructions on how to do this.

Connecting to a Different Device

Select Delete connection with device in the menu in the main view. The app will automatically search for devices once you delete the current connection. You can also start a new search by selecting Connect to device in the menu.

Notes on Heart Rate Devices

Devices may continue transmitting even when removed. Close the app completely to conserve battery.

What Does Corrate Mean?

"Cor" means heart in Latin, inspiring the name Corrate.
